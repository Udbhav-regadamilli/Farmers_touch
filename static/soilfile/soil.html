Know About the Soil Compositions

We provide all the characteristics of different fruits soil properties...

Guava

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 5.5 to 7.0.

Temperature: 73°F to 82°F(23 - 28 g).

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients, particularly nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Climate: Grown in tropical & subtropical climate.

Custard Apple

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.5.

Temperature: 80 °C to 70 °C

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Climate: Grown under warm and humid tropical climate, with mild winters.

Sapota

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.5.

Temperature: 10 °C to 38 °C

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Climate: Grown under warm and humid climate, such as Coastal climate.

Banana

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 5.5 and 7.0.

Temperature: 15 °C to 35 °C

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Climate: Grown under humid and dry tropical.

Papaya

Soil Texture: Aerated sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 25 °C to 30 °C

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium.

Climate: Grown under sun, warm & humid climate.

Grape

Soil Texture: Aerated sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 25 °C to 32 °C

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., calicum, magnesium, potassium.

Climate: Grown under warm,hot and dry climate.
